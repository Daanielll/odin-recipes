Steak Recipe

Really good Steak recipe

This recipe is the easiest recipe yet the most amazing one you could find in the world.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Porterhouse steak
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Get your grill to 700 degrees
  2. Salt the steak
  3. Put the steak in the grill
  4. Flip the steak after 3 minutes
  5. Put the butter on top and let the steak rest for 10 minutes
  6. Serve and have a great meal!
